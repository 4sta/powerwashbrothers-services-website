FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What is pressure washing?

Pressure washing, also known as power washing, is a cleaning technique that uses high-pressure water spray to remove dirt, mold, algae, and other stubborn stains from various outdoor surfaces.

Can pressure washing damage my property?

When performed correctly, pressure washing is a safe way to clean and restore surfaces. However, using incorrect pressure settings or techniques can damage siding, wood, or other delicate surfaces, which is why it's important to hire experienced professionals.

What surfaces can you clean with pressure washing?

We specialize in cleaning a variety of surfaces including driveways, roofs, patios, siding, decks, and outdoor furniture. Each surface requires different pressure settings for effective and safe cleaning.

How often should I have my property pressure washed?

We recommend pressure washing your property at least once a year to maintain its appearance and prevent buildup of grime and mildew. Properties in areas with high exposure to dirt and foliage might need more frequent cleaning.

Is pressure washing environmentally friendly?

Pressure washing is environmentally friendly. as it primarily uses pressurized water to clean surfaces and can significantly reduce the need for harsh chemical cleaners. We also offer eco-friendly cleaning solutions upon request.

How long does pressure washing take?

The duration of pressure washing depends on the size and type of surface being cleaned. A typical driveway might take 1-2 hours., roof might take up to 5 hours., while a full home exterior clean can take up to a day..
